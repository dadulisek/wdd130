Overview

Purpose

Our purpose here is to give the best white water rafting experience for everyone no matter their previous experience. We offer a variety of options from packages for first timers, to more challenging packages for seasoned rafters. Everyone should have the option to start rafting, and enjoy it long after.

Audience

What do they want?: They want to get their first white water rafting experience, or get a more advanced experience based on their previous experience with white water rafting. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?: They expect to find a way to get their first white water rafting experience, or how to get a more challenging experience for more seasoned rafters. What are their needs that aren't being met?: They can't find a good white water rafting experience provider in their area, so they can't go white water rafting even if they want to go.
